KLAI'S MACARONI RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 lb. elbow macaroni
  • 1 pack Lady's Choice Mayonnaisse (200 ml)
  • 1 can fruit cocktail (15 oz.)
  • 12 oz. pineaple chunks
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 oz.)
  • 3/4 cup raisins
  • 3/4 cup nata de coco
  • 6 oz. cheddar cheese
  • 2 quarts water

Instructions:

  1. Cook the macaroni by boiling water in a pot.
  2. Add the macaroni.
  3. Stir and cook for 7 minutes.
  4. Drain the water and arrange macaroni in a large bowl.
  5. Add fruit cocktail,pineapple,kaong,nata de coco,cheese, and raisins.
  6. Gently mix all the ingredients.
  7. Pour condensed milk and then add Lady's Choice Mayonnaisse.
  8. Fold until all ingredients are well blended.
  9. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  10. Remove from the fridge and serve.Share and enjoy!
